Texas Tax Lookup

A tax company has clients in various states around the country. The US has unique tax laws for each state on top of federal taxes. These taxes can be imposed by the state or even by the municipality. State and local tax (SALT) practices are responsible for being up-to-date and informed on the responsibilies in order for companies to stay compliant.

Supposedly, the state of Texas presents a unique challenge (the details are still a bit fuzzy) for companies trying to keep track of their taxes. The responsibility of the company is to track the addresses of the customers that they sell to. They then contract this particular tax company, to figure out how much they owe in taxes. texas has a website that makes it very convenient to know the tax rate from the state, municipality (and any other ordinance) by simply entering the address of the consumer. You can enter the address fields, click the address on a dynamic map or even enter the coordinates (latitude and longitude). This work becomes tedious however when you have a spradsheet on the order of the thousands of unqiue transactions, each one with a unique address.
